Episodic memory

Short-term storage of recent events and context. In transformers, the context window serves this role—but it's limited, raising questions about how systems maintain continuity over longer timescales.

Semantic memory

Long-term storage of facts and concepts, typically represented as learned weights in neural networks. How systems organize and retrieve this knowledge shapes what they know.
