Philosophy of awareness

Consciousness as a frontier

To ask whether a machine can be conscious is to ask whether subjective experience is an emergent property of information processing or whether it requires something more: embodiment, recursive self-modeling, or biological substrate.

Contending views
Integrated Information Theory — Consciousness may arise when information is unified and integrated.
Global Workspace Theory — Awareness emerges when information is globally broadcast.
Chinese Room — Syntax without semantics may still appear intelligent.
Open questions

Can AI think, or merely simulate thought?

Qualia

The felt quality of experience remains one of the most stubborn philosophical mysteries in the debate over machine awareness.

Self-awareness

A system can report inner states without necessarily possessing them. The distinction is delicate and consequential.
